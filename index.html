IndabaX 2025

IndabaX Tunisia is a local chapter of the Deep Learning Indaba, bringing together professionals and AI enthusiasts to connect, share ideas, and explore the latest advancements in Artificial Intelligence.

This two-day event offers attendees an immersive experience with inspiring keynote talks, interactive workshops, and an intense 20-hour hackathon.

For its 5th edition, the IEEE Sup'Com Student Branch is organizing this event to exceed expectations, providing valuable opportunities to grow your network and enhance your expertise in Artificial Intelligence.
